1Overview

ExpenseInk ("the App", "we", "us") is a personal expense and income tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We built ExpenseInk with a privacy-first mindset: your financial data is stored locally on your device by default, and it only leaves your device when you explicitly choose to sign in or back it up.

By downloading, installing, or using ExpenseInk, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide the App's features:

  • Account information: If you sign up with email, we store your name, email address and a securely hashed password. If you sign in with Google, we receive your basic Google profile information (name, email address) via Google Sign-In.
  • Financial data you enter: Transaction amounts, categories, notes, dates, currency preference, and any other data you manually add while using the App.
  • App preferences: Your selected language, currency, reminder settings, and display preferences.
  • Device & diagnostic information: Basic technical details such as app version and operating system, used only to keep the App working correctly.
  • Google Drive backup data: If you enable backup, an encrypted copy of your local database file is uploaded to a private, app-created file in your own Google Drive account.

We do not collect your contacts, photos, location, browsing history, or any data unrelated to the App's core budgeting functionality. ExpenseInk does not include third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

3How We Use Information

Information collected is used strictly to operate and improve the App, including to:

  • Create and secure your account, and let you sign in across sessions.
  • Record, categorize, and display your transactions, statistics, and reports.
  • Generate Excel export files that you choose to create and save.
  • Back up and restore your data to/from your own Google Drive, at your request.
  • Send local reminder notifications to help you log expenses consistently.
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues, and improve app performance and reliability.

4Storage & Security

ExpenseInk is built offline-first. All your transactions and account details are stored in a local, on-device database (SQLite) that is not accessible to other apps. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using industry-standard cryptographic methods before being saved.

Data only travels off your device when you actively choose to sign in with Google or trigger a Drive backup/restore. We use secure, encrypted network connections (HTTPS/TLS) for any such transfer.

5Google Sign-In & Drive Backup

ExpenseInk's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • We request the minimal drive.file scope, which only grants access to files that ExpenseInk itself creates in your Google Drive — we cannot view, modify, or access any other files in your Drive.
  • Your backup file contains only your ExpenseInk transaction database and is never shared with any third party.
  • You can disconnect Google access at any time from your Google Account security settings, or by uninstalling the App.

6Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial information. Data is only shared in these limited circumstances:

  • With Google, solely to enable Sign-In and Drive backup features you explicitly enable.
  • When required to comply with a valid legal obligation, court order, or governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or the App.

7Notifications

ExpenseInk may send local, on-device reminder notifications (for example, a daily nudge to log your expenses). These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external push-notification server or data collection.

8Children's Privacy

ExpenseInk is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9Your Rights & Choices

You are always in control of your data:

  • Access & export: Export your complete transaction history to Excel at any time from within the App.
  • Delete: Delete individual transactions, or uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data from your device.
  • Backup control: Enable or disable Google Drive backup at any time from the App settings.
  • Revoke access: Revoke ExpenseInk's Google account access anytime via your Google Account permissions page.

10Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device for as long as you keep the App installed. Backup copies remain in your personal Google Drive until you delete them or revoke access. We do not retain copies of your financial data on any server we operate, because ExpenseInk does not use a central server for storing user financial data.

11Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable law. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
