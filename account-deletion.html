ExpenseInk — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Developer: Raghov Apps

This page explains how to delete your ExpenseInk account and associated data. ExpenseInk stores your account and transactions locally on your device. Raghov Apps does not operate cloud servers that store your personal or financial data.

1. Delete your entire account and all app data

Follow these steps to permanently remove your ExpenseInk account from your device:

  1. Open ExpenseInk on your Android device.
  2. Go to Profile (bottom navigation).
  3. Tap Log out and confirm.
  4. Open your device Settings → Apps → ExpenseInk.
  5. Tap Storage → Clear data (or Clear storage).
  6. Alternatively, tap Uninstall to remove the app and all local data.

After clearing app data or uninstalling, your name, email, password hash, transactions, and preferences are permanently removed from that device.

2. Delete data you backed up or exported

Clearing the app does not remove copies you saved elsewhere. Delete these manually if needed:

Google Drive backup (optional)

If you used Google Sign-In to back up your data:

  1. Open Google Drive.
  2. Find and delete the backup file: expense_ink.db.

Exported Excel files (optional)

If you used Export transactions in Profile:

  1. Open your device Downloads folder (or Files app).
  2. Delete any ExpenseInk_Transactions_*.xlsx files you no longer want.

3. Delete some data without removing your account

You can delete individual records without deleting your whole account:

  • Open Transactions, tap a transaction, and choose Delete.
  • Update or remove entries from Profile settings (language, currency, reminders) at any time.

4. What data is deleted vs. retained

Data type Deleted when you clear app data / uninstall Retained by Raghov Apps
Name, email, password (hash) Yes — removed from your device No — not stored on our servers
Transactions (amounts, categories, dates, descriptions) Yes — removed from your device No — not stored on our servers
Language, currency, reminder preferences Yes — removed from your device No — not stored on our servers
Google Drive backup file Only if you delete it in Google Drive Stored in your Google account (Google’s policies apply)
Exported Excel files Only if you delete them from Downloads Not stored by Raghov Apps

Retention period: Local app data remains on your device until you clear app data, uninstall the app, or delete backups/exports yourself. Raghov Apps does not retain copies on company servers.
