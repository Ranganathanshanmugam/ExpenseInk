Current Features

Everything you need to manage your money

ExpenseInk packs powerful money-management tools into a clean, distraction-free interface — built for everyday use.

Smart Expense & Income Tracking

Log expenses and income in seconds with rich categories, notes, and dates — everything stays organized automatically.

Visual Reports & Statistics

Understand your spending with monthly, yearly and custom-range breakdowns, category insights and savings trends.

Multi-Currency Support

Track your money in the currency that matters to you, with accurate formatting wherever you spend.

20+ Languages, incl. RTL

Use ExpenseInk in English, Hindi, Tamil, Chinese, Arabic, Spanish and 15 more languages — including right-to-left layouts.

Secure Google Sign-In

Sign in safely with your Google account or a local password — your credentials never leave your control.

Automatic Google Drive Backup

Back up and restore your entire transaction history to your own Google Drive with a single tap — your data, your cloud.

One-Tap Excel Export

Generate polished, professional Excel reports of your transactions to share, print, or archive whenever you need.

Smart Daily Reminders

A friendly daily nudge helps you build the habit of logging every expense so nothing slips through the cracks.

100% Offline-First

Your data lives securely on your device using a local database, so ExpenseInk works flawlessly even without internet.

Simple by Design
